Investment Philosphy

We invest...

...to create products that become
daily habits by making the things people
do every day easier and more efficient.
...in solutions to problems that have been
empirically proven to exist
even if building those solutions is hard.
...in the earliest stages of a product's
development, often pre-product
and pre-VC.
...in great teams with
unique connections to the markets
they are serving.
...mainly in New York City but are open to exploring all opportunities selectively.

What we want to know about your company:

Market

What is the market your company will compete in? How big is it? How well do you know it? Where will revenues come from? How much are customers spending in that space already or how much are they wasting?

Problem

What is the problem in your market that no one is solving? How do you know this is a real problem? If a solution is developed, why will potential customers care? Will they pay for it?

Solution

What is your proposed solution? Why is it an order of magnitude better than anything else out there? Why is now the right time for this solution to exist?

Competitive landscape

How are customers currently spending money in the space? What are their options when they want to solve this particular problem? How well do you know your competitors? How much attention do you pay to them?

Team

Why are you the right team to solve this particular problem? What is your unfair advantage? How do you grow your team and maintain quality? How well do you understand your strengths and weaknesses as a team? What are your biggest weaknesses/gaps and how you plan to address them?

Fundraising

Why are you raising money? Why specifically from Quotidian Ventures? Why are you raising this specific amount? What are the milestones you plan to achieve between this round of financing and the next? What if things don't go as planned?
